# Operations And Cold Beverage Throughput Notes

## Beyond Chat Alignment - May 4, 2026

Use this as operational risk context for Research, Data, Finance, and decision-brief writing.

Source type: Notion company knowledge

## Operations Concern

Cinder Orange Cold Brew must be validated against peak morning throughput. If the citrus component adds too many partner steps, the product risks slowing the same daypart it is meant to improve.

## Operational Guardrails

- Keep build sequence under five partner actions.
- Avoid garnish requirements in drive-thru unless prepped.
- Prefer syrup or infusion format over fresh squeeze.
- Keep RTD and handcrafted versions visually connected.

## Public Grounding

Starbucks has publicly described cold beverage station redesign through the Siren System and highlighted work to reduce time and steps for cold beverages. The company also discussed cold pressed cold brew technology as a way to improve cold coffee preparation.
