# Legal, privacy, and public claims audit

Existing Terms/Privacy/Pricing/Billing pages were intentionally not edited because they contain concurrent user changes and require business/legal facts not present in the repository. Before public launch, counsel and the accountable business owner must approve them.

Required Terms decisions: contracting entity/contact/address, eligibility, organization authority, acceptable use, customer content/license, connected apps and third-party terms, AI limitations/no professional advice, payment/tax/refund/cancellation terms, suspension/termination, warranty/liability/indemnity, dispute law/forum, changes, and beta terms.

Required Privacy decisions: controller/contact, categories/sources/purposes/legal bases, processors/model providers, cookies/analytics, organization-admin visibility, retention, deletion/access rights, international transfers, children, security limits, US state rights, and change notices. Publish a current subprocessors/data-handling list and deletion contact/process.

Claims must match shipped depth. Do not claim live paid checkout, enterprise readiness, compliance certifications, unlimited usage, complete connectors, SSO/SCIM availability, guaranteed accuracy, zero retention, encryption details not verified, production Sentry/OTel, completed recovery drills, or 500-seat validation until evidence exists. `$30/user/month` may be described only as the planned price while checkout remains disabled. Billing success must say “verification pending” until the backend confirms subscription state.
