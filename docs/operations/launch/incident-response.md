# Incident response and communication

Declare severity and incident commander; open a timestamped internal record; preserve evidence; stop harm with the narrowest kill switch; rotate/revoke exposed credentials; and communicate verified facts only. For billing incidents, disable checkout/portal or entitlement consumption independently, preserve provider/internal events, and never bulk-grant access as remediation.

Every update states impact, affected window/cohort, current mitigation, user action if any, and next update time. Avoid speculation and sensitive identifiers. Notify legal/privacy/payment owners when required. Recovery requires service validation, reconciliation, monitoring, and explicit commander approval. Complete a blameless postmortem with timeline, contributing controls, detection, customer impact, corrective owners/dates, and evidence that fixes were tested.

The incident drill remains an external gate until a timed tabletop and technical kill-switch exercise are witnessed and recorded.
