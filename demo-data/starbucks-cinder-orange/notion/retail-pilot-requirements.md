# Retail Pilot Requirements

Source type: Notion company knowledge

## Pilot Scope

- 400 stores
- 8 weeks
- West, South, California, Texas, Florida, and New York Metro priority clusters
- App promotion in week 1 and week 3
- No national paid media until pilot readout

## Store Readiness

Required before launch:

- recipe card
- partner training clip
- ingredient prep guide
- POS and app tile QA
- cold beverage station timing check
- signage kit

## Decision Metrics

- units per store per week
- repeat rate
- attach rate
- cannibalization index
- gross margin
- store partner complexity score
- NPS and product sentiment

## Stop Conditions

- stockout rate exceeds 8%
- ops complexity score exceeds 4.5
- repeat rate stays below 20%
- cannibalization index exceeds 25%
- margin falls below 65%
