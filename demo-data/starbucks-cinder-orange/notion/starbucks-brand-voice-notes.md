## Brand Principles

- Lead with coffee, craft, and the customer ritual.
- Make flavor feel sensory and specific.
- Keep innovation premium but accessible.
- Avoid clinical energy, health, or performance claims.
- Do not make a Starbucks drink sound like soda, juice, or a canned energy product.

## Voice For Cinder Orange

Preferred language:

- bright
- smooth
- citrus peel
- cold brew
- summer morning
- lightly sweet
- coffee-forward
- refreshing ritual

Avoid:

- extreme energy
- metabolism
- detox
- soda-like
- candy orange
- medical hydration

## Demo Copy Direction

Cinder Orange Cold Brew should sound like a Starbucks cold coffee innovation, not a novelty beverage. The product should communicate a familiar base with a seasonal citrus finish.

## Public Grounding

Starbucks publicly describes cold as a defining shift in beverage behavior and says cold beverages shape global innovation. Starbucks also emphasizes craft, store experience, and getting back to the core Starbucks experience.
