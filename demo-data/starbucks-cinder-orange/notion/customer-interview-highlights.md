# Customer Interview Highlights

## Beyond Chat Alignment - May 4, 2026

Use this as customer-language context for Writing, Research, and Compare. The product should pass this as context rather than hardcoding it.

Source type: Notion company knowledge

## Positive Language

- "I want cold brew that feels lighter in summer."
- "Orange peel sounds more premium than orange syrup."
- "If it still tastes like coffee, I would try it."
- "I would order it on a hot morning before work."
- "It needs to look like Starbucks, not like a canned soda."

## Objections

- "Orange and coffee sounds risky."
- "I do not want it to be too sweet."
- "If it tastes like juice, I am out."
- "I would need to understand whether it is coffee-first."

## Implication

The winning positioning is `bright citrus cold brew`, not `orange coffee`. The creative should show orange peel and cold brew together so the customer understands both parts quickly.
