## Pipeline Note

Cinder Orange is the strongest fit for a summer pilot if the decision goal is cold coffee growth rather than afternoon refreshment. It keeps the launch tied to coffee and the morning daypart.

## Decision Criteria

- Can the product defend premium price?
- Can store partners make it fast?
- Does it expand cold coffee without stealing too much from core cold brew?
- Does the visual system work across app, menu, and bottle?
