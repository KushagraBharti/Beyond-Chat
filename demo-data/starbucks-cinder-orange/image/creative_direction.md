# Creative Direction

## Visual System

- Starbucks green foundation
- warm orange accent
- white negative space
- cold condensation
- glass and bottle reflections
- orange peel, not orange juice
- coffee beans or cold brew darkness for coffee-first read

## Must Communicate

- premium
- refreshing
- coffee-first
- summer morning
- limited-time pilot

## Must Avoid

- soda
- juice
- energy drink
- overly sweet dessert cue
- generic cafe stock imagery
