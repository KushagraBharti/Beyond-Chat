# Product Launch Proposal

## Proposal

Proceed with a controlled 400-store pilot of Cinder Orange Cold Brew, focused on warm-weather regions and morning commuter channels.

## Rationale

The product connects to three public Starbucks priorities:

- cold beverage momentum
- disciplined menu innovation
- operational consistency

The concept also creates a clear test of whether Starbucks can stretch cold brew into a seasonal summer ritual without losing coffee credibility.

## Research Questions

- Does citrus increase trial without creating taste confusion?
- Can the product preserve cold brew margin?
- Which channel drives the best repeat behavior?
- Does the RTD version support or dilute the in-store launch?
