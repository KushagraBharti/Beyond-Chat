# Pilot Decision Brief Draft

Artifact type: saved Beyond Chat artifact
Studio: Writing

## Decision

Should Starbucks approve a 400-store, 8-week summer pilot for Cinder Orange Cold Brew?

## Current Read

Approve a controlled pilot if the team can hold gross margin above 68%, keep store partner complexity below 4.0, and position the drink as coffee-first.

## Why Now

Cold beverage demand gives Starbucks permission to test more seasonal cold coffee ideas. The concept is differentiated enough to create app and menu visibility while still connected to the core cold brew platform.

## Open Questions

- Which regions over-index on warm-weather cold coffee trial?
- How much core cold brew cannibalization is acceptable?
- Can the citrus component be operationally simple?
- Does the RTD bottle read as coffee or juice?
