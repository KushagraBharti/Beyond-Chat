## Executive Summary

Cold beverage innovation remains strategically important for Starbucks because cold now represents the majority of beverage behavior in important markets. Cinder Orange Cold Brew is credible because it extends cold coffee with a seasonal sensory hook while remaining close to Starbucks' core coffee identity.

## Category Signals

- Cold coffee continues to shape customer behavior.
- Seasonal beverages create urgency and app-friendly merchandising.
- Fruit cues work best when they support a coffee platform instead of replacing it.
- Retail bottled pilots need especially clear packaging hierarchy.

## Recommendation

Proceed to data and finance analysis. The concept is strong enough for evaluation but should be pressure-tested for cannibalization, preparation complexity, and gross margin.
