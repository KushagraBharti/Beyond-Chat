# Prior Cold Brew Launch Learnings

Artifact type: saved Beyond Chat artifact
Studio: Research

## Summary

Prior cold brew launches succeeded when they combined a familiar coffee base with one specific seasonal cue. Customers wanted novelty, but only after the product clearly remained coffee.

## Useful For Cinder Orange

- Keep the cold brew base explicit.
- Use orange peel, zest, or brightness rather than orange juice language.
- Show the drink in a morning setting.
- Avoid over-indexing on afternoon refreshment unless paired with an app promotion.

## Launch Rule

If the product cannot be explained as `cold brew plus bright citrus`, it is too complex for pilot launch.
