# Product FAQ

## What is Cinder Orange Cold Brew?

A seasonal cold brew with a bright citrus finish designed for summer mornings.

## Is it coffee-first?

Yes. The product should lead with cold brew and use orange peel or citrus brightness as an accent.

## Is this a Refresher?

No. The product belongs to the cold coffee platform, not the Refreshers platform.

## What is the main risk?

Customers may misunderstand orange and coffee unless the creative system clearly communicates cold brew first.

## What does success look like?

Strong repeat rate, limited cannibalization, attractive margin, and manageable store complexity.
